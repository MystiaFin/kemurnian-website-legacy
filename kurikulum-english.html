KEMURNIAN SCHOOL ENGLISH PROGRAM

1. INTRA-CURRICULAR PROGRAM

1. TK (Kindergarten)

  • The kids have both school and native teachers.
  • School uses Cambridge Curriculum with Cambridge books, Super Safari, for teaching and learning materials.
  • Native teacher accompanied with an assistant and school teachers use the same Cambridge books to teach the kids.
  • The students obtain two progress reports from school and Native program.
  • For Kemurnian Citra Kindergarten, the school has applied Bilingual program.

2. SD (Primary School)

  • Students have school English teachers as well as native teachers that teach four Language skills : Listening , reading, speaking and writing.
  • School English teachers teach two language skills, listening and reading as well as Grammar.
  • Native teachers teach two other language skills, speaking and writing
  • School English and native teachers use the same Cambridge Books, Super Minds as the main teaching resources or materials
  • The Cambridge English books are equipped with teacher's book, active-teach (interactive teaching with multi-media), class audio, assessment books .
  • Students obtain school progress report and certificate of native program from English Plus as school English program partnership.
  • All of the sixth grade students are to take Cambridge exam of Level A2.

3. SMP (Junior High School)

  • Students have school English teachers as well as native teachers that teach four Language skills : Listening , reading, speaking and writing.
  • School English teachers teach two language skills, listening and reading as well as Grammar.
  • Native teachers teach two other language skills, speaking and writing
  • School English and native teachers use the same Cambridge Books, Think as the main teaching resources or materials
  • The Cambridge books, Think are equipped with teacher's book, active-teach (interactive teaching with multi-media), class audio, assessment books .
  • Students obtain school progress report and certificate of native program from English Plus as school English program partnership.
  • All of the ninth grade students are to take Cambridge exam of Level B1.

4. SMA (Senior High School)

  • Students have school English teachers as well as native teachers that teach four Language skills : Listening , reading, speaking and writing.
  • School English teachers teach two language skills, listening and reading as well as Grammar.
  • Native teachers teach two other language skills, speaking and writing
  • School English and native teachers use the same Cambridge books, Think as the main teaching resources or materials
  • The Cambridge books are equipped with teacher's book, active-teach (interactive teaching with multi-media), class audio, assessment books .
  • Students obtain school progress report and certificate of native program from English Plus as school English program partnership.
  • g. All of The twelfth grade students are to take Cambridge exam of Level B2.

2. EXTRA-CURRICULAR PROGRAM

  • Prepare and practice students for their English performances in all school events such as Year-End party, Graduation Day, Kemurnian Cup, Chinese New Year , Christmas , etc. ( TK -SMA )
  • Participate in any English Competitions held by other schools , Universities or English Institutions.
  • School internal English Competitions. (TK - SMA)
  • Student short presentations and dramas for taking students' final test marks. (SD - SMP)
  • English Club (SMP)
  • Toefl Class, IELTS class and English Speaking Club with native teacher (SMA)
  • Toefl test with students' Toefl score Certificate (SMA)
